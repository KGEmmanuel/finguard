# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@cognitagrc.io**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant is available at the link above and is incorporated here by reference.
